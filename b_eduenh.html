Enhancing Your Education

State Farm and other similar companies regularly have internships available for high school students. I plan on interning at State Farm this Summer. State Farm has numerous departments filled with internship opportunities that are usually chosen by the intern.
I was able to tour some departments of StateFarm with my Computer Science class on a fieldtrip and found out that I was familiar with the tasks they were doing.
I have also participated in Hour of Code over the past years that has also helped me understand coding more.
Additionally, taking summer courses of coding can easily increase one's knowledge about coding. I took a 20 hour long course over the summer, CodeQuest, and learned so many new things. I had to use Github, and I learned how to write algorithms, create a website, make a Chrome extensions, build an Alexa skill (RockPaperScissors), and attempted AWS databases. A skill this course taught me was to be patient when errors occur. Before, I had no idea where to even look when I had errors. Now, I can easily find my error and fix it without getting irritated. This course has also expanded my creativity, I never thought I could make a website because of the time I have to put into it. But I've become fascinated with it. Website designing has also taught me to be very detail-oriented. How a couple words and numbers can create a useable website for everyone to use or even an app.
